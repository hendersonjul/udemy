About the dog

  • Group: Working
  • Size: 23-27.5 inches
  • Weight: 70-115 pounds
  • Lifespan: 7-10 years

Top facts about the Bernese Mountain dogs diet

  1. They need high quality food with relatively low protein level, approximately 18 -26% and a moderate fat content, under 16%.
  2. Many Bernese love eating a variety fresh food.
  3. Small additions of yogurt, cooked or lean raw meat, fish , eggs or fresh fruits, including banana, apple or pear and vegetables, including carrots, broccoli, squash, cooked pumpkin or yams can be offered as a way of introducing enzymes to a diet.
